Welcome to Foodstore Gwenny!

Where culinary excellence meets convenience!

In august 2022 Gwenny Casier and Kurt Senaeve (owners of "Vishandel Traiteur Jonas") had the idea to expand their store, this time a little closer to home.
They wanted to create a store where you can buy fresh products and other small items you can easily forget.
In February 2023 they opened Foodstore Gwenny in the center of Eernegem.

Foodstore Gwenny is a store that is open 24/7 and there are only vending machines.
You can find a wide range of products in our store. All these products are homemade in the kitchen of "Vishandel Traiteur Jonas".
Foodstore Gwenny is located at Stationsstraat 305, Eernegem 8480.
